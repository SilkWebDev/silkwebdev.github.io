Services

We follow the principle that no job is too small.

Whether the extent of your project is building a small wall, installing a new kitchen or refacing old brickwork - up to building new houses on empty plots - we offer our competitive services just the same.

Listed below is some of the work we regularly undertake. It is not an exhaustive list, so please feel free to contact us with your project even if you don't find it here.

We can also provide a free estimate based on either your specification and our inspection, or architectural and structural drawings.

Managing your project

We can provide complete management of your project, from start to finish.

All of our quotes include a full breakdown of the costs of each phase of the project. We are completely up-to-date with current building regulations and ensure your project meets the regulation standards.

Importantly, we work alongside you in a transparent manner. We ensure that throughout all stages of the building process that you are kept informed and value your input at any stage of development.

When necessary, we liase with any architects, engineers and building control for inspections and the final sign-off.

In addition, we source the best materials within the constraints of your budget.

Interior works

We offer a variety of internal works that include but are not limited to:

Various carpentry jobs such as kitchen fitting, changing or fitting or internal doors, skirting and architrave.

In addition, installation of staircases, wooden flooring and click-system floors, and floor tiling.

We also have extensive experience in demolition and removal of internal walls and installation of steel beams to create open-plan layouts.

Exterior works

We offer a variety of external works that include but are not limited to:

Groundworks of all types such as digging foundations, concreting and installation of drainage.

Landscaping of all types including patios, paving, decking and building of garden walls.

Pointing and/or refacing of old brickwork or rendering.

We also have extensive experience in demolition and removal of external walls and installation of steel beams and bifold doors to create a seamless transition from your home into your outdoor areas.

Sourcing materials

We leverage our long-standing business relationships with local building merchants to allow us to secure you the most competitive rates on building materials.

Similarly, we have good relationships with local building reps that ensure we have a wide variety of potential suppliers to draw from.
